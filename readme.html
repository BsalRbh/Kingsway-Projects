How To Play

Gameplay:

  1. Players: The game is played between two players.
  2. Choices: Each player simultaneously chooses one of three possible items: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  3. Outcome: The winner is determined by the rules:
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

How to Play:

  1. Step 1: Choose any option rock, paper or scissors
  2. Step 2: Click on the chosen option.
  3. Step 3: The Computer will randomly choose on option .It will compare the rules to determine the winner or if it's a draw.
  4. Output: It will show the result and the scores are also recorded.
  5. Reset Score: You can simply click on reset scores button to reset the scores.

Enjoy playing! 🎮👊✋✌️
